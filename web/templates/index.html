Hirschy Factor Guide

Efficiency Index (EFF)

The core health of a team. Measures point-per-possession margin Adjusted for SOS. The higher the weight, the more 'Chalk' the simulation reflects.

Free Throw Advantage (FT)

Measures a team's ability to capitalize on easy points while limiting opponent opportunities. Critical for close game simulations.

Defensive Premium

Hyper-weights defensive efficiency. High values favor elite defensive units even if they struggle offensively.

The SOS Multiplier

Applies a toughness coefficient. Teams with higher SOS get a win probability boost against teams with 'inflated' records.
